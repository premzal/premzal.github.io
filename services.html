Meet the Team

UCSD ECE 196 Fall 2022

The hardworking team behind SprinkleBot formed in the Electrical Engineering ECE 196 course at UC San Diego. Congregating in the Structural Engineering building across from the Envision Makerspace Studio, our team brainstormed and produced the design for an automated plant watering system. We consist of two electrical enineering and one computer engineering student, all focusing on specific areas of this project. Steve led the PCB design, hardware assembly, and presentations. Prem handled the website, sensor coding, and workshop proposal. Raymond managed firebase server setup, video editing, and equipment sourcing. Our efforts combined make SprinkleBot the renowned system it is known as today.
