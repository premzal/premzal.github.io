Features

The SprinkleBot Team has designed a custom PCB Board to accomodate all of our sensors utilized for the microprocessor, and to ensure that the customer has a error-free user experience. We have a temperature, humidity, light, and soil moisture sensor which all provide information about the plants surrounding environment and soil in order to provide it with ample hydration. In addition, our system sends data to online database in real time so that a customer may monitor metrics of their plants environment from anywhere in the world! The project is coded in python using pre-build libraries for the sensors and raspberry pi that we are using, needs two power sources (a wall outlet and 9V battery), and requires a stable wifi connection in order to transmit data to the database. Our system has an anti-flooding feature wherein the user can press the emergency stop button on the system to stop the pump from providng wateer, in the case of a pump malfunction or over-watering of plant.
